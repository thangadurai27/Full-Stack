About Us

Green Leaf Cafe At Green Leaf Cafe you will experience genuine hospitality and a cultured atmosphere. One can appreciate the unforgettable ingredients and respect for simplicity with each item on the menu and our daily specials. ​ Step into the Green Leaf Cafe and indulge in a delightful culinary experience that goes beyond mere sustenance; because healthy, good food is what we believe in. Savor our menu of handmade sandwiches, fresh salads, soups, and other items that are carefully crafted with love and attention to detail. Our house made desserts will transport your tastebuds to a realm of pure bliss and where every bite is a moment of sheer delight. If you're in the mood for a delectable snack, don't miss our multiple flavors of kettle corn, handheld treats and more that are placed throughout the market. We offer a variety of gluten free and allergen friendly options! ​ Green Leaf is more than just a place to satisfy your cravings. It's a vibrant social hub, where friends gather, laughter fills the air, and new connections are forged. Whether you're seeking a cozy spot to work, a haven for relaxation, or a refreshing break from shopping at the Marketplace, our Cafe is here to welcome you with open arms. So come on in, enjoy a mouthwatering meal, and make lasting memories at Green Leaf Cafe!

From Our Hearts to Your Plate

Having run a preschool for more than ten years, we were seeking a new business opportunity that would offer a different pace and atmosphere. Our aim was to focus on our family and engage in activities that we enjoy. As we are fond of coffee, music, and socializing with pleasant individuals, we decided to open a coffee shop where we could experiment with food and beverage concepts and have a good time with our loved ones and community.

The Best Bite in the Neighborhood

The Green Leaf came into existence in 2016, but unfortunately, we had to cease operations by the end of 2019 due to health concerns and the pandemic. However, after nearly four years, we are thrilled to announce that we are reopening at our new location, where we can once again share delicious food, warm smiles, and engaging conversations with our community.
